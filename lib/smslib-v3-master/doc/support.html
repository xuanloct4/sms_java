Support

SMSLib News

Important news and other announcements about new releases, bugs/bug fixes, and general chitchat about the library are posted on the News page. News are also posted on Twitter.

SMSLib Discussion Group

The SMSLib Discussion Group is available for your queries and feedback. A Google Account is required in order to participate. Posts from new members are moderated, so there might be a slight delay if you post for the first time.

Issue Tracker

The Issue Tracker is also available for reporting issues, bugs or filing enhancement requests.

Repository

You can find the SMSLib source repository here: https://github.com/smslib/smslib-v3
